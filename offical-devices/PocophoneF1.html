Installation Instructions

1. Backup All Data with your beloved backup Apps.
2. Download AncientOS , choose between weeabo/primeval build vanilla or gapps variant. All build include FaceUnlock and doesn't include inbuilt firmware.
3. Update to suggested firmware 11.0.8.0 or newer, else you don't need to update.
4. Do reboot to recovery, and go to wipe menu. Check system, data, vendor, dalvik, cache.
5. Then, Flash ROM > Gapps (optional) > DFE > reboot to system
6. After booted successfully, send screenshot to group :D.
